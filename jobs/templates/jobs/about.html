Our journey

With close to ten years of experience sending students to Germany for a higher education, EduOptions Germany is a one-stop solution that facilitates quality, educational experience in Germany. With more and more students opting to study in Germany, we provide a holistic solution that will enable students to realize their dream of a foreign education. To this end, EduOptions Germany is continuously expanding its portfolio to include more study programs for aspiring students – from Studienkolleg, to undergraduate, postgraduate, short-term courses and vocational training, at institutions around Germany.

With offices in Mumbai and Pune and affiliates across the country, EduOptions Germany is introducing more and more students to the wonders of a foreign education and experience. With over hundreds of students having successfully passed through our offices, EduOptions Germany is an ideal partner for universities, students and affiliates alike. With a team of dedicated, trained and experienced staff, EduOptions Germany provides an individual experience, depending upon each student’s goal, interest and aspirations.

At EduOptions Germany, we aim to help you obtain a world class education while making the experience memorable and the process as seamless as possible by guiding you every step of the way.
